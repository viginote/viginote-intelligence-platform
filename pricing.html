Common questions

How does billing work?
Monthly subscriptions are billed on the same date each month. Annual subscriptions are billed upfront and save you 17% compared to monthly. Payment is handled via invoice — contact info@viginote.com to get started.
Can I change my regions or streams after subscribing?
Yes — contact your analyst and we'll update your profile within 24 hours. Region and stream changes take effect at your next billing cycle unless you're upgrading, in which case they're instant.
What are intelligence streams?
Streams are topic-based intelligence filters on top of your geographic coverage. Geographic is always included (conflict, security, military). Additional streams cover Maritime Security, Cyber & Infrastructure, Economic & Financial Risk, Political Risk, and Executive Protection. You choose which streams match your risk profile.
What counts as an intelligence brief?
A brief is a formally structured intelligence document generated from selected alerts — covering key judgement, risk matrix, situational overview, analyst assessment, and recommendations. Formatted for client distribution and downloadable as PDF.
Is there a contract or minimum term?
Monthly subscriptions have no minimum term — cancel anytime. Annual subscriptions are 12-month commitments. Bespoke arrangements are negotiated individually.
Who is VigiNote for?
Security consultants, corporate travel managers, risk analysts, NGO field directors, private equity due diligence teams, law firms with international exposure, and any organisation that needs actionable geopolitical intelligence without a six-figure enterprise contract.
